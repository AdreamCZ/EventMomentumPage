Privacy Policy for EventMomentum

Effective Date: 20.02.2025

EventMomentum (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we access, use, and handle your information when you use our service, which connects to your Google Calendar to plan Facebook and Instagram posts based on your calendar events.

1. Introduction

By using EventMomentum, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

2. Information We Access

Google Calendar Data:

  • Scope: EventMomentum connects to your Google Calendar with your explicit permission. We access the event data (such as event names, dates, times, and descriptions) solely for the purpose of planning your social media posts.
  • Limited Use: We do not store or retain your Google Calendar data. Once we have used this data to generate and schedule your Facebook and Instagram posts, the data is immediately discarded.

3. How We Use Your Information

The sole purpose of accessing your Google Calendar events is to:

  • Plan Social Media Posts: Analyze your calendar events to suggest or schedule relevant Facebook and Instagram posts.
  • Temporary Processing: Process your event data in real time and then discard it without storing it on our servers or any database.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Ephemeral Processing: EventMomentum processes your Google Calendar data in-memory. The data is not written to disk, stored in databases, or kept on our servers.
  • Discarding Data: Once the relevant social media posts have been planned, your Google Calendar data is permanently discarded and is not retrievable by us.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your Google Calendar data with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively for the purpose described above.
  • Service Providers: We may work with third-party service providers solely to facilitate the connection to your Google Calendar or the scheduling of your social media posts. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and are not permitted to use it for any other purpose.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the temporary processing of your Google Calendar data:

  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted between your device and our service is encrypted using modern encryption protocols.
  • Access Controls: Access to your data is restricted and monitored to ensure that it is only used for its intended purpose.

7. Third-Party Services

EventMomentum uses third-party services (such as Google APIs, Facebook, and Instagram platforms) to perform its functions. These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend that you review them:

  • Google: Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook: Facebook Data Policy
  • Instagram: Instagram Privacy Policy

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our practices.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Information: Request information about the data we have processed on your behalf.
  • Revocation of Consent: Disconnect your Google Calendar from EventMomentum at any time, which will immediately halt the processing of your calendar data.
  • Questions and Concerns: Contact us with any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices.
